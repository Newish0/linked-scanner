Features

Wireless Scanning

Use your phone's camera as a barcode scanner — no extra hardware needed.

Plug and Play

Cross-Platform

Windows, macOS, and Linux desktop app + a PWA that runs in any mobile browser.

Peer-to-Peer

Direct WebRTC connection (PeerJS) for a fast, low-latency scanning experience.
